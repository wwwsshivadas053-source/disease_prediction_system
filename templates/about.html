{{ _("About MedVision AI") }}

AI support for faster, clearer skin-image screening.

MedVision AI helps users upload skin images, receive AI-assisted predictions, understand possible disease context, and download structured reports. It is designed as a decision-support tool, not a replacement for a doctor.

01

{{ _("Image Analysis") }}

The platform accepts uploaded skin images and uses a trained model to estimate the most likely disease class with a confidence score.

02

{{ _("Disease Guidance") }}

Each result includes disease-specific explanation, visible warning signs, and practical next steps so the output is easier to interpret.

03

{{ _("Reports & History") }}

Users can track previous predictions, review confidence levels, and download PDF reports for personal records or clinical discussion.

{{ _("Important Medical Note") }}

AI predictions can be affected by lighting, blur, camera angle, skin tone representation, and model limitations. If a lesion is new, changing, painful, itchy, bleeding, or visually concerning, consult a qualified healthcare professional even if the AI result appears low risk.
